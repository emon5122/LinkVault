LinkVault is a fast, private, offline-first place to save every link that matters — and actually find it again.

Save an article, video, tool, or recipe in a tap. LinkVault automatically pulls in the page title, description, preview image, and site icon so your library looks clean without any manual work. Everything is stored locally on your device: no account, no sign-up, no servers, no ads.

SAVE FROM ANYWHERE
• Paste a URL, or share a link from your browser or any app
• Automatic preview: title, description, image, and favicon from the page
• Duplicate detection warns you before saving the same link twice
• "Save copied link?" prompt when you copy a URL

ORGANIZE YOUR WAY
• Folders with custom icons and colors
• Multiple tags per link
• Favorites, Read Later, and Pinned collections
• Archive links you're done with — restore or delete anytime
• Markdown notes with a live preview

FIND ANYTHING INSTANTLY
• Instant search across titles, URLs, descriptions, notes, folders, tags, and sites
• Highlighted matches so you spot the right link fast
• Sort by newest, oldest, title, or most visited
• List or grid view

READ LATER, YOUR WAY
• A dedicated Read Later queue
• Optional daily and weekly reminders
• Open links in the built-in browser or your default browser

IMPORT & EXPORT — YOUR DATA IS YOURS
• Import from CSV, JSON, or browser bookmark (HTML) files
• Export to CSV, JSON, or Markdown
• Full local backup and restore

BEAUTIFUL & FAST
• Clean, minimal design with light, dark, and system themes
• Smooth animations, swipe actions, and haptics
• Built for speed, even with thousands of links

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
LinkVault keeps everything on your device. There are no accounts, no tracking, and no analytics. Page metadata is fetched directly from the sites you save. Your bookmarks are yours.
